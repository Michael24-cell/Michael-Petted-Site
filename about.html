Michael Petted: Artist and Educator

A mission to turn craft into art, one scene at a time.

The Educator

Michael Petted has been a dedicated acting teacher since **2009**, spending ten years shaping the craft of hundreds of actors at the renowned **Beverly Hills Playhouse**.

He is deeply familiar with the industry, having also served as the Head Acting Instructor for the **Hollywood Immersive** program for eight years, helping international actors bridge the gap between training and professional work.

Today, Michael teaches his own scene study and on-camera acting class at **The Hudson Theatre in Los Angeles**, focusing on truth, artistry, and practical technique for actors at every level.
